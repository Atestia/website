We maintain a minimal sub-processor footprint. All sub-processors are bound by DPAs (Data Processing Agreements). EU/UK transfers are governed by SCCs (Standard Contractual Clauses) where applicable. We provide 30-day advance notice for any sub-processor addition or material change.

Change notifications

When we add a sub-processor, change a sub-processor's role, or change a sub-processor's location of customer data processing, we notify customers at least 30 days in advance by email and by updating this page. Customers may object to a new sub-processor within 30 days of notice as their contractual right.

To receive sub-processor change notifications by email, send a request to legal@atestia.ai. We maintain a mailing list of customer legal/privacy contacts for this purpose.
