Solutions — Audit firms

Become a Tier-4 Certified Auditor.

The Tier-4 Compliance Working Group is establishing the third-party auditor program for the Tier-4 specification. Big-4 audit firms and boutique IP firms can join as approved auditors and certify their bank clients' implementations annually.

Why your bank clients need this

SR 26-2 entered effect April 17, 2026. The OCC examination cycle under the new guidance begins in 2027. Banks are scrambling to demonstrate runtime evidence capture for every vendor model their agents invoke. Your engagement teams are the people they trust to validate it.

REVENUE OPPORTUNITY

$50K–$250K per cert

Annual Tier-4 certification fees will scale with implementer size. The Working Group is targeting a standard revenue-share split with Approved Auditor firms. No partnership agreements have been executed yet; the program opens for partner applications when the 501(c)(6) filing closes Q3 2026.

PROVEN MARKET

Every Tier-1 bank

Banks invoking vendor models at scale need certification or face supervisory exposure. The market is the entire $30B+ AUM segment.

FIRST-MOVER

Founding cohort

First 3 audit firms to join the program (targeted Q1 2027 opening) will be invited to co-design the certification methodology and will receive named placement in the Approved Auditor registry. No firms have been confirmed; this is a public invitation.

The certification program

LEVEL 1

Implementer

Self-declared. Implementation passes basic schema conformance. Listed in the public Registry. No audit required.

Fee: $0

LEVEL 2

Conformant

Implementation passes the Conformance Test Suite. May use "Tier-4 v1.x Conformant" descriptor in marketing.

Fee: $5K test suite. Annual.

LEVEL 3

Certified

Third-party audit by an Approved Auditor. Annual renewal. The spec aims to produce evidence at examiner-grade depth; whether a specific regulator extends deference is determined by that regulator, not by the spec.

Fee: $50K–$250K depending on implementer size.

Apply to become an Approved Auditor

The first 3 Approved Auditor seats open in Q1 2027. Eligibility criteria:

  • ✓ Existing Model Risk Advisory or comparable practice
  • ✓ Demonstrated banking sector audit engagements
  • ✓ Class II General Membership in the Tier-4 Compliance Working Group (or higher)
  • ✓ Trained auditors who complete the Tier-4 Methodology certification
  • ✓ Adequate professional liability coverage
