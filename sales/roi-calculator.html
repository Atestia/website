Methodology

Baseline (manual reconstruction): Industry benchmark — 1 OCC 2013-29 packet requires ~120 MRM analyst hours when reconstructed from CloudWatch logs, vendor SOC 2 reports, email threads, and Confluence pages. SR 26-2 attestation packs require ~200 hours. BCBS 239 lineage queries vary; we model 40 hours per examiner question.

With Atestia: Packet generation is automated and reviewed (~4 hours of MRM analyst review per packet for QA and bank-specific footnotes). Lineage queries take minutes.

Atestia pricing: Year 1 ACV based on bank AUM tier — Tier-1 ($100B+ AUM): $1.5M to $3M; Regional Tier-2: $250K to $750K; Community: $50K to $150K. The calculator selects pricing automatically from your AUM input.

NPV calculation: 3-year horizon, 10% discount rate, 5% Year-2 contract uplift, 3% Year-3 uplift. Pilot fee credits toward Year 1 production.

Uncosted benefits: Regulatory action risk reduction (which has tail-risk magnitudes of $10M-$50M+ in CFPB consent decrees per recent precedent), reputation premium with examiners, downstream Tier-1 audit firm reception, and MRM analyst attrition reduction are not modeled.
