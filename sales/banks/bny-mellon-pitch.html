The gap

Governance is documented. Evidence is not yet captured at runtime.

The 16-step flowchart describes the policy. The flowchart does not capture what actually happened at runtime in production across 20,000 agents on a Tuesday at 3pm.

Today

  • · Agent decisions tracked in 16 review steps
  • · Audit log: who pressed approve when
  • · What the agent actually invoked: distributed across Azure logs, Gemini admin console, internal tool logs
  • · Vendor model attestation: reconstructed manually when examiner asks
  • · SR 26-2 packet for examination: ~3 weeks of analyst time

With Atestia

  • · Same 16-step governance flow
  • · Plus runtime lineage: every vendor model call captured
  • · Plus reasoning chain: the thinking flow that led to each decision
  • · Plus 5 packet generators: OCC 2013-29, SR 26-2, BCBS 239, ECOA Reg B, custom
  • · Examiner packet generation: 4 hours, on demand
