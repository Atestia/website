Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-06-02. Last updated 2026-06-02.

Atestia is operated by FuteurCredX Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Atestia," "we," "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have under GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and other applicable privacy laws.

1. What we collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Contact information when you book a demo, apply to the Working Group, or email us: name, email, company, role, phone if provided.
  • Account information if you subscribe to Atestia Pro: email, billing address, payment method (processed by Stripe; we never store card numbers).
  • Communications you send us (support requests, sales inquiries, Working Group correspondence).

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Website analytics: page views, referrer, browser type, IP-derived region. Only after you accept cookies.
  • Atestia Pro usage: API call counts, tool invocations, error rates. Used for billing and platform reliability.

1.3 What we explicitly do NOT collect

  • Customer payload data. The Atestia Tier-4 MCP server captures hashes of vendor model inputs, never raw inputs. Bank customer records, transaction details, and PII never reach Atestia infrastructure.
  • Vendor model outputs are not stored. Only metadata (vendor ID, agent ID, latency, success/failure) is captured.
  • Banker LLM prompts are not collected. The reasoning capture tool stores agent-supplied summaries only.

2. How we use information

  • Deliver and operate the Atestia Platform and Atestia Pro service.
  • Respond to sales inquiries, schedule demos, and process Working Group applications.
  • Bill subscriptions and process refunds.
  • Improve the product and the open-source reference implementation.
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax records, regulatory inquiries, court orders).
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse of our services.

3. Legal bases (GDPR)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract: to provide services you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests: to run our business, prevent fraud, and improve products.
  • Consent: for non-essential cookies and marketing communications.
  • Legal obligation: where required by law.

4. Who we share data with

We share limited personal data only with:

  • Service providers under data processing agreements: AWS (US-East-1 cloud), Stripe (billing), Cloudflare (DNS+email), Vercel (web hosting), Vanta or Drata (SOC 2 control automation).
  • Professional advisors: legal counsel, accountants, auditors under confidentiality.
  • Authorities: when required by binding legal process or to protect rights, safety, or property.
  • In connection with corporate transactions: merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with customary protections.

We never sell personal data and have never received money or other valuable consideration for personal information.

5. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete your data (right to erasure / right to be forgotten).
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Receive a portable copy of your data.
  • Opt out of "sale" or "sharing" (CCPA/CPRA — we do not sell, but you may still opt out).
  • Withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent).
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Submit requests to privacy@atestia.ai. We will respond within 30 days (45 days for CCPA, extendable once with notice).

6. International transfers

Our primary infrastructure runs in AWS US-East-1 (Virginia). If you access our services from outside the United States, your data will be transferred to and processed in the US. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU/UK data transfers and supplementary measures as required by the EU GDPR and UK GDPR.

7. Retention

  • Contact form submissions: 2 years from last interaction.
  • Atestia Pro account data: for the duration of your subscription plus 7 years for financial records.
  • Atestia Platform tenant lineage data: customer-controlled. Default retention 7 years per SR 26-2 examination requirements; customer may instruct earlier deletion subject to retention obligations.
  • Marketing analytics: 26 months maximum.
  • Working Group membership records: while membership active plus 3 years (IRS recordkeeping for 501(c)(6)).

8. Cookies

We use a minimal set of cookies. The banner you saw on first visit lets you accept or reject non-essential cookies. Strictly necessary cookies (session, security, language preference) are set without consent and cannot be disabled.

9. Children

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact privacy@atestia.ai and we will delete it.

10. Security

We use encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256 with AWS KMS). Production access is logged. Multi-factor authentication is required for administrative actions. We will pursue SOC 2 Type 1 in 2026 and SOC 2 Type 2 in 2027. Report suspected vulnerabilities to security@atestia.ai.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced via the home page banner or by email to active subscribers at least 30 days before taking effect.
