About

Banks have shipped agentic AI faster than they shipped the evidence layer.

Atestia exists to close that gap. We build the runtime evidence layer for regulated AI. We sponsor the open Tier-4 specification. We are the reference implementation maintained by an industry association banks can trust.

Mission

Every Tier-1 US bank has deployed agentic AI in production. None has shipped the corresponding evidence layer. When the next examination cycle begins, examiners will ask for vendor model attestation and the banks will not have an answer.

We are building the answer. Open spec. Open source reference implementation. Commercial enterprise tier for production banks.

Values

  • Open by default. Standards are CC-BY-4.0. Reference implementation is MPL-2.0. Working Group governance is independent.
  • Banks know what they need. We don't impose architecture; we publish specs and reference code banks can adopt or extend.
  • Evidence is product. A regulator-ready packet is the deliverable, not a feature.
  • Privacy is non-negotiable. Atestia never stores raw payloads, only hashes.
